Our Journey

From Idea to Reality

The story of how ETCETRA came to be.

2020
The Beginning

ETCETRA started as a simple idea in Alex Johnson's apartment. Frustrated with the lack of high-quality oversized clothing options, Alex decided to create a brand that would fill this gap in the market.

With just a few designs and a small investment, ETCETRA was born. The first collection consisted of just three t-shirt designs, produced in limited quantities.

2021
Growing Pains

As word spread about our quality and design, demand quickly outpaced our production capabilities. We faced challenges with scaling while maintaining our commitment to quality.

This year marked our expansion into a proper studio space and the addition of key team members who would help shape the future of the brand.

2022
Breakthrough

2022 was our breakthrough year. Our signature oversized tee was featured in several major fashion publications, and our online presence grew exponentially.

We established partnerships with ethical manufacturers and began sourcing organic cotton exclusively, aligning our production with our values.

2023
Expansion

With a solid foundation in place, we expanded our product line to include hoodies, sweatpants, and accessories, all maintaining our signature oversized aesthetic and quality standards.

We also launched our first physical pop-up stores in major cities, allowing customers to experience our products in person.

Today
Looking Forward

Today, ETCETRA is recognized as a leader in premium oversized clothing. We continue to innovate and expand, but our core values remain the same: quality, sustainability, and style.

We're excited about the future and grateful for the community that has supported us along the way. The best is yet to come.
